SpokaneOS is a friendly group of Apple platform developers that meets once a month. Our aim is to hang out, get to know each other, and enjoy tasty food and beverages while fostering a welcoming local tech community.

Not an Apple developer? No problem! We welcome anyone who’s interested in connecting with us, including developers on other platforms, managers, designers, and entrepreneurs.

Want to say hi before attending a meetup? Join our Spokane Tech Discord and introduce yourself in #general. If you want to dig into Apple tech discussions right away, chat with us in #🍎-swift.

We meet every 2nd Wednesday at Saranac Commons where there’s a coffee shop, vegan/gluten-free bakery, pizza place, and brewery.

Parking is paid. Don’t want to drive a car? There are bikes racks to store your bike and ample bus lines that lead to this centrally located space. Everything is on the first floor and accessible.

We’re looking forward to getting to know you!
